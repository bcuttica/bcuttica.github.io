Chicago entrepreneur, Startup Institute Student

 

Brian is an entrepreneur looking to do something he cares about and make an impact.

In year 6 of what ended up being an 8 year career in finance Brian started to get interested in the idea of startups. The prospect of working on something new, making an impact, and actually enjoying work was an appealing idea.

Brian co-founded Revolving Collections Gallery and left his corporate job in January of 2013 in order to pursue an entreprenuirial career.

To help push this new career forward Brian enrolled in the Startup Institute in October 2013.

Brian is learning to be a full stack Technical Marketer while at SIC and would love to bring an anylitical marketing approach to a startup he really believes in.
